Rolex

SERVINGS:
4
CALORIES:
200
PREP:
10 min
COOK:
15 min

QUICK FIX, EAT HOT, TASTY

A rolex is simillar to a vegetable omelet. Cabbage, tomato, and onion are cooked with eggs and then rolled into a chapati, a tortilla-like unleavened flatbred.

INGREDIENTS

Eggs 2
Onions 1 small/half medium
Tomatoes 1 medium/half large
Cooking oil 2/3 teaspoons
salt 1 pinch
chapati on request

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Chop the onions and tomatoes into small pieces.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and add the onions and tomatoes.
  3. Beat the eggs in a bowl and add salt to taste.
  4. Cook until the onions are translucent.
  5. Add the eggs to the pan and cook until the eggs are set.
  6. Roll the eggs into a chapati or tortilla.
  7. Enjoy
